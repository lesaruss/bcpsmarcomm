Website & Social Media Implementation Plan

Academic Year 2026–2027 | Executive Overview

Purpose

Establish a consistent, district-aligned approach to school websites, social media, and enrollment engagement that strengthens each school’s ability to tell its story, build trust, and guide families from first impression to enrollment.

Websites = official source of information

Social Media = daily storytelling and engagement

Schools = final experience through Enrollment Inquiries and Tours

All must work together to move families from awareness to inquiry to enrollment

Deliverables: Overarching Expectations

1

Content Alignment & Storytelling

  • Messaging aligned to school story and strengths (School Blueprint Document)
  • Aligned to BCPS Advantage initiative (monthly emails with templates)
  • Aligned to district branding expectations (MarComm Website)
  • Content reflects authentic student learning experiences and outcomes
2

School Website Readiness

  • Pre-School Year Requirement
  • Key components updated before August 2026
  • Required: homepage with identity banner, news, calendar, CTA; current Magnet/Innovative/Specialized Program pages; updated Proud Points
  • Supports enrollment inquiries and tour scheduling
  • Information is accurate, family-friendly, and current
3

Social Media Presence

  • Baseline Requirement
  • Every school maintains at least one active social media account
  • Post consistently
  • Highlight school culture, academics, and programs
  • Include clear calls to action: visit website, schedule a tour, apply
  • Drive families to website and inquiry/tour actions
4

Enrollment Inquiry & Tour Readiness

  • Timely inquiry responses and welcoming on-campus experience
  • Contact information is visible and accurate
  • Staff are prepared to respond and engage families
  • Tour experience reflects the story told online
  • Online and in-person experiences must match

Monthly Implementation Timeline

July 2026 (Pre-Launch)
  • Finalize all website updates
  • Distribute Semester 1 Social Media Content Calendar

Includes BCPS Advantage initiative posts

Guidance on enrollment inquiry expectations and school tour readiness

Key Expectations for Success

  • Websites fully updated before August 2026
  • Every school has at least one active social media account
  • Content shows real student learning and experiences
  • Every post includes a clear call to action: tour, inquire, apply
  • Schools respond to and manage enrollment inquiries effectively
  • Schools provide welcoming, consistent tour experiences
  • Messaging aligned across website, social media, and in-person

Decisions / Support Needed from Executive Team

Leadership endorsement and active support required across three key areas to ensure consistent implementation district-wide.

Endorsement of:

  • Website readiness requirement (August 2026)
  • Requirement for active social media presence
  • Enrollment inquiry and school tour expectations framework

Support for:

  • Regional monitoring and accountability
  • Training and reinforcement for schools

Alignment on:

  • BCPS Advantage messaging integration
  • District branding and communication standards

Closing

This plan ensures that all schools enter the 2026–2027 academic year with aligned messaging, strong digital presence, and clear enrollment pathways for families.

Goal: Provide clarity, alignment, and confidence that schools are fully prepared to engage families, support enrollment, and deliver a consistent experience from first impression through campus visit.

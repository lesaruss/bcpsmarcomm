Broward County Public Schools

Web Content Manager (WCM) Scope of Work

Updated: June 24, 2026 | 2:14 PM – 2:21 PM ET Scope updated following WCM assignment module deployment and operational workflow review. Incorporates meeting transcripts from Finalsite Call and Department Guidelines Planning sessions.

Executive Summary

The Web Content Manager (WCM) program establishes standardized website governance across Broward County Public Schools. School principals designate WCMs who complete certification coursework and maintain compliance with BCPS website standards.

WCM Assignment & Certification Workflow

Principal-Level Assignment: Principals log into BCPS Central, select their school, and designate WCMs from existing staff or new users. System automatically sends welcome email with course enrollment link. All assignments timestamped with administrator identity for audit trail.

  • Confirm existing WCMs from prior school year
  • Add new WCMs via staff search or manual entry
  • Automated welcome email with dynamic school/WCM details
  • Bulk upload capability currently in development

Certification Status Tracking

Four-Stage Certification Model:

  • Not Registered: WCM designated, awaiting course enrollment
  • Registered: Enrolled in WCM certification course, in progress
  • Incomplete: Working through course modules, not yet certified
  • Certified: Course completed and certification achieved

Each certification includes start date (enrollment) and end date (expiration). Principals see read-only status; central admin updates status as WCMs progress.

Multi-Year Audit & Compliance Management

Challenge: Schools may carry incomplete audit items from prior years (2025-26). These must be surfaced without cluttering current year view.

Solution Implemented:

  • Outstanding items remain segregated in prior school year
  • Year selector dropdown at top of audit page organizes view
  • Clear cross-year alert system alerts schools with pending items
  • Report center provides administrative cross-year visibility
  • Estimated 60-100+ schools currently have unresolved 2025-26 items

Checklist & Status Tracking:

  • All schools receive audit checklist per school year
  • Central admin manually updates school compliance status as items are checked off
  • Three terminal statuses: Pass, Incomplete, Fail
  • Automated status computation script in development (rules being finalized)
  • Bulk reporting and update interface planned

Role-Based Access Control

Office of Communications (OOC):
Access: MarComm Portal, Graphics Library, Minutes Archive, Annual Reports
Role: District-level communications and branded materials management

Department Web Team (DWT):
Access: MarComm Portal, WCM Certification Module, Minutes Archive, Annual Reports
Role: Technical implementation and cross-district coordination

Super Admin:
Access: System configuration, permissions panel, advanced settings
Role: Limited to Sean Russell and designated district leadership

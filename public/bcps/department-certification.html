Department Certification Process

Overview

The Department Certification Process ensures that all BCPS departments maintain compliance with district standards for web accessibility, content management, and organizational governance.

Certification Steps

Step 1: Initial Audit

Department website undergoes accessibility and content audit to identify gaps and requirements.

Step 2: Remediation

Department works with Web Team to address audit findings and implement required changes.

Step 3: Verification

Web Team verifies all changes meet district standards and accessibility requirements.

Step 4: Certification

Department receives official certification of compliance valid for 12 months.

Recertification

Departments must undergo recertification annually to maintain compliance status.

Support

For assistance with the certification process, contact the District Web Team.

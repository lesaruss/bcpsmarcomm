Connect - Learn - Lead

Strengthening Our WCM Community. Together we connect, learn, and lead - and tell the story of BCPS.

6
Leave with commitments, connections, and a clear game plan for the year ahead.

The Top 10 Countdown - Schools

#10
Content Cleanup and Audit
A fresh start means a clean site. Review outdated pages, broken links, archived documents, and stale staff listings. We will walk through the content audit process and the district tools available to identify what needs to go before the year begins.
#9
Accessibility and ADA Compliance
Accessible content is not optional. We will cover the key requirements that apply to every school page - what the most common failures are, and how to fix them before they become issues.
#8
Calendar Connections
Your calendar is one of the highest-traffic elements on any BCPS school page. We will cover how to connect events, sync with district calendars, format entries for readability, and make sure families never miss a date.
Activity Break
Quick Networking Prop: Turn to someone you have not met today and share your biggest web content challenge from last year. You have 90 seconds. Go.
#7
Photography and Media Standards
Images and video make or break a first impression. We will review the district photo standards, what makes a good school photo, how to format and compress images for the web, and the proper use of student media in line with district policy.
#6
School News and Announcements
Timely, well-written announcements keep families informed and reduce phone and email overload. We will cover the announcement format, how to use the news module effectively, and best practices for headlines, summaries, and calls to action.
#5
Home Page Banner Best Practices
The banner is the first thing anyone sees. We will cover what belongs in a banner, what never should be there, how to write effective banner copy, and how to rotate content strategically throughout the year.
Activity Break
Stretch Break + Mini Poll: Stand up, stretch, then cast your vote on today's most surprising countdown item so far.
#4
Home Page and Media Best Practices
Beyond the banner - we will walk through the full school homepage and how to structure it for families coming in for the first time. Covers layout, essential modules, the sidebar, and media placement that works.
#3
Marketing Toolkit Integration
The Marketing Toolkit is your shortcut to on-brand, district-approved content. We will do a live walkthrough of everything in the toolkit - templates, graphic standards, copy guidelines, and how to request support from the communications team.
Team Challenge
60-Second Challenge: Your table has 60 seconds to name as many BCPS website modules as possible. Team with the most wins a prize.
#2
WCM Community and Collaboration
You are not in this alone. We will spotlight the WCM peer network, introduce this year's shared resource channels, and give everyone a chance to meet and connect with WCMs from schools across the district.
#1
Future of BCPS Digital Communications
The most important countdown spot. We will share what is coming in the year ahead - new tools, new district web initiatives, and what WCMs need to know to stay ahead of the curve. The future is bright and you are part of it.
10:00 AM

Live demo of all three toolkits you will use this year. Know where to find them, how to use them, and who to contact when you need support.

Community

Toolkits You Will Leave With

Marketing Toolkit
Brand assets, templates, and copy guidelines for all BCPS school content. Your shortcut to on-brand publishing.
ADA Toolkit
Accessibility checklist, remediation tools, and compliance resources. Stay WCAG 2.2 AA compliant all year.
WCM Toolkit
Finalsite guides, video tutorials, and the WCM quick reference card. Everything you need, in one place.

Web Content Manager (WCM) Scope of Work

Updated: June 24, 2026 | 2:14 PM – 2:21 PM ET Scope updated following WCM assignment module deployment and operational workflow review.

Executive Summary

The Web Content Manager (WCM) program establishes standardized website governance across Broward County Public Schools. School principals designate WCMs who complete certification coursework and maintain compliance with BCPS website standards.

WCM Assignment & Certification Workflow

Principal-Level Assignment: Principals log into BCPS Central, select school, and assign WCMs. System sends welcome email with course enrollment link. All assignments timestamped with administrator details.

  • Confirm existing WCMs from prior school year
  • Add new WCMs via search or manual entry
  • Automated welcome email with dynamic content
  • Bulk upload capability in development

Certification Status Tracking

System tracks four distinct certification statuses:

  • Not Registered: WCM designated, awaiting enrollment
  • Registered: Enrolled in course, in progress
  • Incomplete: Working on course modules
  • Certified: Course completed, credential valid

Multi-Year Audit & Compliance Management

Schools may carry incomplete items from prior years. Outstanding items remain in prior year view; year dropdown keeps data organized. Clear cross-year alerts planned. Approximately 60-100+ schools currently have 2025-26 incomplete items requiring resolution.

Role-Based Access Control

  • OOC: MarComm, Graphics, Minutes, Reports
  • DWT: MarComm, WCM Module, Minutes, Reports
  • Super Admin: System configuration and permissions

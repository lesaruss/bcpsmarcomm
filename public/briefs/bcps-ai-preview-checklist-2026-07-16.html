What's held out of this preview

Per the prior session's flags, nothing below was touched. Each needs a decision from BCPS or from Sean before it goes in.

Our Principles page vs. the real AILF

BCPS's real 8-domain Artificial Intelligence Leadership Framework (AILF, tied to the Florida Educational Leadership Standards) was read in full this session, but the current generic nine-principle page was left alone. Needs a call on whether the AILF fully replaces it, a parent-friendly summary replaces it with the full document linked, or it stays a separate leadership-only resource.

Copilot student access wording

BCPS's own documents disagree with each other (the framework outline says AI-Academy pilot only; ISD PMO Sections.docx says broader grade-based access). The existing generic "role-based student access controls" line on the live Approved Tools card was left as-is rather than guessed at. Needs an answer from whoever owns the Copilot rollout.

Digital Learning Blueprint disclosure

The Ethical Implementation Guide is confirmed as an active, unapproved 60-plus page "Review Draft," not a finished policy. None of its competency content was pulled into the preview. Needs a decision on disclosure language before any of it is used.

District and School Leadership one-sheet placement

This one-pager's content is finished and ready to use as written, but where it belongs is a placement call, not a content gap: a standalone "For Staff and Leaders" resource, or folded into Parent Resources as the leadership companion to the parent one-pager. Left out until that's decided.

Full nav restructure

BCPS's own SharePoint folder order (Landing Page, Our Principles, Approved Tools, Parent Resources, Learning, Community) still doesn't match the live site's eight-tab nav one-for-one. The AI Liaison Network tab was added in the right relative position, but a full restructure was intentionally not done this session, per the content map's own recommendation to hold that until the decisions above are in.

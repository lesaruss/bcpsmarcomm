Executive Summary

The Web Content Manager (WCM) program establishes standardized website governance across Broward County Public Schools. School principals designate WCMs who complete certification coursework and maintain compliance with BCPS website standards.

WCM Assignment and Certification Workflow

Principal-Level Assignment

Principals log into BCPS Central, select their school, and designate WCMs from existing staff or new users. The system automatically sends a welcome email with a course enrollment link. All assignments are timestamped with administrator identity for audit trail purposes.

  • Confirm existing WCMs from prior school year
  • Add new WCMs via staff search or manual entry
  • Automated welcome email with dynamic school and WCM details
  • Bulk upload capability currently in development

Certification Status Tracking

Not Registered
WCM designated, awaiting course enrollment
Registered
Enrolled in WCM certification course, in progress
Incomplete
Working through course modules, not yet certified
Certified
Course completed and certification achieved

Each certification includes a start date (enrollment) and end date (expiration). Principals see read-only status; central admin updates status as WCMs progress.

Multi-Year Audit and Compliance Management

Cross-Year Item Management

Challenge: Schools may carry incomplete audit items from prior years (2025-26). These must be surfaced without cluttering the current year view.

  • Outstanding items remain segregated in prior school year
  • Year selector dropdown at top of audit page organizes view
  • Clear cross-year alert system notifies schools with pending items
  • Report center provides administrative cross-year visibility
  • Estimated 60-100+ schools currently have unresolved 2025-26 items

Checklist and Status Tracking

  • All schools receive audit checklist per school year
  • Central admin manually updates school compliance status as items are checked off
  • Three terminal statuses: Pass, Incomplete, Fail
  • Automated status computation script in development (rules being finalized)
  • Bulk reporting and update interface planned
